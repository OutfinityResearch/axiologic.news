About TTS Voices

Text-to-Speech voices vary by browser and operating system. Chrome typically offers the widest selection. The selected voice will be used for all wizards that have TTS enabled (Quiz, Educational, RSS, Philosophy, etc.).

For best results, use clear, simple text and avoid complex formatting or special characters.
